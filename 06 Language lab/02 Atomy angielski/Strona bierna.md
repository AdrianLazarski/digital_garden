## Czasowniki modalne

### W czasie teraźniejszym i przyszłym

|                                                                                                                          |     |                               |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | --- | ----------------------------- |
| can/can't  <br>could/couldn't  <br>may/may not  <br>might/mightn't  <br>should/shouldn't  <br>ought to  <br>must/mustn't | be  | past participle  <br>(III for |

It's the kind of secret that shouldn't be told to anyone.
Michael may be given a promotion.

### W czasie przeszłym

#### Przypuszczenia
czasownik modalny + have been + III forma
She couldn't have been murdered earlier. The traces say otherwise.
The missing documents may have been stolen.

#### Konieczność lub jej brak

had to/didn't have to  + be + III forma
could/couldn't + be + III forma

- The report had to be finished on time so we worked like mad till midnight.
- The professor arrived on time and the lecture didn't have to be cancelled.
